18 Vintage Christmas Cookies

Description

In preparation for the holiday season, these delicious Christmas cookies will be enjoyable for all!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Get ingredients
  2. Mix Dry Ingredients
  3. Mix wet ingredients and then combine the wet ingredients with the dry ingredients until forming a smooth dough
  4. Form into small balls about 20g each. Bake in the oven at 350f for about 12 minutes
