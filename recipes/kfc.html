Korean Fried Chicken

Decription

How to make sweet, spicy and sticky Korean fried chicken! It's super crunchy and hugely addictive!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Cornstarch
  • Gochujang
  • Cooking Oil
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Cut chicken into even pieces
  2. Coat the chicken with cornstarch and seasoning
  3. Fry chicken for about 20 minutes flipping halfay.
  4. Take chicken out of pot and toss in Gochujang sauce.
